CFHSTC

CFB Borden
Located at Canadian Forces Base Borden, the Canadian Forces Health Services Training Centre (CFHSTC) is the primary training establishment for Regular and Reserve Force members of the Canadian Forces Medical Services and the Royal Canadian Dental Corps. The Training Centre was stood up as an amalgamation of the Canadian Forces Medical Service School and the Canadian Forces Dental Services School. The Canadian Forces Health Services Training Centre uses timely, innovative, cost-effective, and pertinent training methodologies to educate and train our Canadian Forces Health Services professionals.
